ACCOMMODATIONS AND TOURS

Tokyo provides a variety of accommodation choices. The conference venue is surrounded with more than 3000 rooms within a walking distance. For those on a budget, Tokyo has many great hostels and guest houses that provide both safety and comfort. There are also capsule hotels, some of which are women-only. For more privacy, there are mid-tier and business hotels that provide convenience and all the basic amenities, making a great option for travelers who plan to spend most of their time out exploring the city. Luxury hotels in central Tokyo provide excellent 5-star service, city views and views over the water if you decide to stay in the Tokyo Bay area.

JTB Global Marketing & Travel Inc. (JTBGMT) has been appointed to the official travel agent for ICC2019 and will handle accommodation.
JTBGMT has booked rooms at hotels in Tokyo for this conference period. Reservations will be processed in order of application.
*Capsule hotels are not provided by the official travel agent.
